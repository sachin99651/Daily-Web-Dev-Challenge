Revolutionary Breakthrough in Sustainable Energy

Local scientists discover a way to harness ambient static electricity for home use.

By Sachin Kumar

In a stunning turn of events yesterday, researchers at the Institute of Advanced Technology announced a discovery that could change the face of global energy consumption. The team has successfully developed a prototype that captures low-level static charges from the surrounding atmosphere.

While the technology is currently in its early stages, the implications are vast. Experts suggest that within a decade, homes could be powered entirely by the invisible energy that surrounds us. This breakthrough marks a significant shift toward a truly carbon-neutral future.
